🪙 How to Buy

  1. Install a Solana wallet (Recommended: Phantom, Solflare).
  2. Purchase SOL on a centralized exchange (such as Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken).
  3. Transfer SOL to your Solana wallet address.
  4. Connect your wallet to a Solana DEX (Recommended: Jupiter, Raydium).
  5. Swap SOL for Carota Canina (Paste the official Carota Canina token address in the DEX, select the amount, and complete the swap).
  6. Done! Your Carota Canina tokens will appear in your wallet. Enjoy your new Roman dog coins!
